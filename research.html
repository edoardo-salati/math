Research

My main interest lies in the interplay between group theory and homotopy theory, with a focus on tools and techniques related to the classification of the finite simple groups and to the Martino-Priddy conjecture (now a theorem).\\ I am currently investigating fusion systems, localities and, more generally, partial groups. The manipulation of partial groups naturally leads to the study of (symmetric) simplicial sets and to questions that have more of an homological and homotopical algebra flavor.
