I am a research associate at the RPTU Kaiserslautern-Landau in Kaiserslautern, currently a member of the group of Gunter Malle.
Previously I have completed by PhD at the Technische Universität Dresden under the supervision of Ellen Henke. The focus of my thesis was on building a result, analogous to a theorem of Meierfrankenfeld, Stellmacher and Stroth, for fusion systems and localities, aiming to obtain a description of their p-local structure. Since then my research scope has widened to encompass more of the homotopy theory of fusion system, which I approach by studying the simpicial structure of localities and partial groups. Current collaborates are Justin Lynd, Philip Hackney and Rémi Molinier.
I have completed my Bachelor and Master Studied at the Università degli Studi di Milano Statale. Both my Bachelor and Master dissertations were supervised by Emanuele Pacifici. The former revolved around techniques for the investigation of finite groups that were key tool in the classification of the finite simple groups, the latter was about supercharacter theories and their lattices.
